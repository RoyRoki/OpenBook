Key Features

User Authentication

  • Login and Logout: Easily log in to your account with secure authentication methods and log out with a single click.
  • Secure Password Management: Change and update your password at any time using robust security protocols.

Post Creation and Interaction

  • Text Posts: Share your thoughts, ideas, and updates through text posts.
  • Engagement: Like, comment, and share posts to interact with your friends and followers.

User Profile Management

  • Profile Customization: Personalize your profile with bio, profile picture, and other personal information.
  • Privacy Controls: Manage who can see your posts and profile details with comprehensive privacy settings.

News Feed

  • Real-time Updates: See the latest posts from people you follow in a dynamic and chronological feed.
  • Trending Topics: Stay informed with trending posts and topics from the wider community.

Security Features

Encryption

  • Data Encryption: All user data, including posts and personal information, is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
  • Password Encryption: Passwords are hashed and salted to ensure maximum security.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

  • Extra Layer of Security: Add an additional layer of security with 2FA, requiring a second form of verification during login.

Regular Security Audits

  • Vulnerability Scanning: Routine security checks and vulnerability assessments to ensure the platform remains secure against threats.
  • Penetration Testing: Regular penetration testing to identify and fix potential security issues.

User Privacy

  • Data Control: Users have full control over their data, with easy options to download or delete their information.
  • Anonymity Options: Post anonymously in certain sections to protect your identity if desired.

Secure Development Practices

  • Code Reviews: Thorough code reviews and adherence to secure coding practices.
  • Compliance: Compliant with industry standards and regulations, such as GDPR, to protect user data.
